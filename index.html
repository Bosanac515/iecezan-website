About Us

A center built by community, for community

IEC Ezan is a non-profit Islamic center proudly serving Muslims in the greater Des Moines area. We are a place for daily prayer, religious education, cultural connection, and service to those in need.

Faith

Daily prayers, weekly Jumu'ah, and a welcoming space for worship for Muslims of every background.

Education

Mekteb classes for our children, Qur'an study, and programs that connect generations to their faith and heritage.

Community

Eid celebrations, weddings, funerals, and events that bring families together and welcome new neighbors.
